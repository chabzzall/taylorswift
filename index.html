Taylor Swift

She is a American singer - songwriter, Her narrative songwriting, which often centers around her personal life, has received widespread critical plaudits and media coverage.

Her 2006 eponymous debut studio album was the longest-charting album of the 2000s on the Billboard 200. Its third single, "Our Song", made her the youngest person to single-handedly write and perform a number-one song on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart.
